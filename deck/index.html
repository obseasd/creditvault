THE PROBLEM

DeFi on Creditcoin is fragmented

For Users

  • Must manually research & evaluate each yield source
  • Capital sits idle or is concentrated in a single protocol
  • No auto-compounding — missed yield from unclaimed rewards
  • Multiple transactions needed to diversify across strategies

For the Ecosystem

  • TVL is scattered across isolated protocols
  • No composable yield layer for other protocols to build on
  • New DeFi protocols struggle to attract capital
  • Lack of standardized vault interfaces (ERC-4626)

Creditcoin needs a unified yield layer that aggregates capital, optimizes returns, and provides a composable building block for the ecosystem.

WHY CREDITCOIN

The Ideal Chain for DeFi Infrastructure

Scalable L1

Creditcoin is a purpose-built Layer 1 for real-world financial infrastructure. Its EVM compatibility and high throughput make it perfect for DeFi primitives like composable vaults.

EVM Compatible

Full EVM support means battle-tested standards like ERC-4626, ERC-20, and OpenZeppelin contracts work natively. No need to reinvent — deploy proven DeFi patterns directly.

Growing Ecosystem

Creditcoin's ecosystem is expanding with new DeFi protocols. CreditVault is positioned to be the composable yield layer that connects and amplifies them all.

Low Fees, Fast Finality

Affordable transaction costs make frequent operations like harvest() and rebalance() economically viable — critical for auto-compounding vault strategies.

CreditVault brings Yearn-style yield aggregation to Creditcoin

The first composable yield vault on the network — ready to integrate real protocols as they launch.

CEIP ALIGNMENT

Investable DeFi Infrastructure

CreditVault directly addresses the Creditcoin Ecosystem Investment Program goals.

Composable Yield Layer

Any CTC holder can earn diversified yield without managing multiple positions. ERC-4626 cvCTC shares are composable building blocks for the entire ecosystem.

TVL Growth Engine

A single deposit distributes capital across multiple protocols. More strategies means better yield, more deposits, and more total ecosystem TVL — a positive flywheel.

Pluggable Architecture

IStrategy interface means zero vault changes when real protocols launch on Creditcoin. Deploy a new strategy adapter and the vault seamlessly redirects capital.

Production Quality

24 tests (including fuzz), 8 verified contracts, full-featured frontend, auto-compounding. Not a prototype — a production-grade DeFi primitive ready for mainnet.
